The reference design from Canon.com stands out for its visually captivating and user-friendly approach, embodying Canon's dedication to innovation. The website's seamless interface, intuitive navigation, and responsive design across devices contribute to a positive user experience. Noteworthy features include a harmonious color scheme, high-quality imagery, and the incorporation of interactive elements, setting a benchmark for modern web design. In my modified design, I aim to draw inspiration from Canon's strengths while introducing tailored enhancements to further optimize the user interface and overall visual appeal.
